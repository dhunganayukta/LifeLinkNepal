Why Choose Us

Built for the Critical Moment

Every feature of LifeLink Nepal is designed around one question — how fast can we get blood where it's needed?

Instant Alerts

The moment a hospital posts a blood request, compatible donors within reach receive a real-time notification — no delay, no middleman.

Verified Network

Every donor and hospital in our network is verified before they can participate, ensuring trust and safety at every step of the process.

Live Tracking

Hospitals can monitor donor responses in real time. Donors see their impact logged with every contribution they make to the community.

The Process

From Request to Rescue

Four straightforward steps that can mean the difference between life and loss.

I
Hospital Posts

Hospital logs an urgent blood request with type and units needed

II
Donors Notified

Compatible donors near the hospital receive instant priority alerts

III
Coordination

Donors confirm availability and connect directly with the hospital

IV
Life Saved

Blood reaches the patient. Donation is logged. A life continues.
