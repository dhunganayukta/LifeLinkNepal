Our Mission

Bridging the Gap
When Seconds Count

In Nepal, emergencies shouldn't be a gamble on social media reach. We saw families losing precious time posting on Facebook, waiting for "shares" while their loved ones were in surgery.

Social media algorithms don't prioritize life-and-death situations — but we do. LifeLink Nepal connects hospitals directly with verified blood donors in real-time, eliminating the chaos of emergency blood searches.

Every feature we build is guided by one question: how fast can we get blood where it's needed? That relentless focus is what sets us apart.

The Platform

How Life Link Works

Designed for fast, life-saving action — from first post to blood delivered.

Step 01
Hospital Posts Request

The hospital logs an urgent request — blood type, units needed, urgency level. It takes under a minute.

Step 02
Donors Are Notified

Our system instantly alerts compatible verified donors nearby — ranked by proximity, compatibility, and availability.

Step 03
A Life is Saved

The donor confirms, arrives, and donates. The request is fulfilled. The donation is logged. A family goes home together.
